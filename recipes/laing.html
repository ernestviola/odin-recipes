Laing

Description

A traditional Filipino dish from the region of Bicol. It's a dish perfect on top of rice to add some nice flavor. It's made out of dried taro (purple potato) leaves and coconut milk.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups coconut milk
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 8-10 pieces of dried or smoked fish
  • 5 cups of dried taro leaves
  • 3 pieces of chile pepper, chopped and seeded

Steps

  1. Bring to boil in a pot over medium heat the coconut milk, water, onion, garlic, and dried fish.
  2. Once the pot comes to a boil add the taro leaves. Cover with a lid and let boil for 10 minutes.
  3. Add the chiles. Season with salt to finish.
  4. Let boil until the liquid has reduced or almost completely gone.
