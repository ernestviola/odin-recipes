Shakshuka Recipe

Description

Shakshuka (or shakshouka) is a traditional Tunisian dish featuring poached eggs in a spicy tomato sauce with onions, bell pepper, and garlic.

I sometimes throw whatever I have available into the dish but the essentials are tomato and eggs.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 1/3 cups chopped onion
  • 1 cup thinly sliced bell peppers
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 2 1/2 cups chopped tomatoes
  • 1 hot chile pepper, seeded and finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon of groud cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 4 large eggs
  • Optional cheese. I prefer a soft goat cheese.

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Mix in all of your vegetables that you want to cook except for the tomatoes.
  2. Throw in your mixed dry ingredients into the skillet and let the vegetables simmer for about 5 minutes.
  3. Pour in your tomato mixture and make sure to mix while scrapping off any of the stuck parts at the bottom of the skillet.
  4. After the tomato juices have reduced make 4 pockets in the shakshuka and crack your eggs into them. Cover the skillet for a few minutes to let the eggs cook. This is also a great time to add some cheese.
  5. After about 4 minutes take it off the stove and bring it to the table. It's great to have with some bread.
